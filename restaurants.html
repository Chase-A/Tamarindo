Restaurants in Tamarindo

Black Stallion

Our Ranch style BBQ has become very popular for friends & families to relax & enjoy an exclusive BBQ buffet cooked under the stars of Guanacaste. We BBQ ranch chicken, ribs, steak, pork, veggies & more. A very delicious experience with lots of food served with salads, sangria, beer & wine & tropical desert.

Dragonfly Bar and Grill

Dragonfly consistently delivers exceptional cuisine, fantastic wines, cocktails and craft beers with gracious and knowledgeable service. Sourcing fresh local ingredients and seasonally adapting our menu options, Dragonfly offers creative culinary cuisine in a charming and romantic atmosphere.

El Mercadito

"Best food I had in Costa Rica and at a good price was the fried red snapper dinner at Mariscos. You have a choice of many types of food at Mercaditos, but you should try Fernando ar Mariscos at least once."

La Baula

"The playground is an awesome feature for kids while waiting on a table or for the food to come. The menu is nothing but pizza and one type of salad but all delicious. The pizzas are thin and crispy. We really liked the prosciutto arugula and parm."

Longboards BBQ

"I had my first Tamarindo meal in Longboards and have been returning for six years. Cold beer, great selection for lunch and dinner options, helpful staff, and daily specials. I'm not a soccer fan but I like the enthusiasm of the sports bar clients as they watch the games on multiple screens. At night there's often entertainment. It feel clean and has a good vibe."

Pico Cafe

"Credit has to be given when it is earned. The service and the food are exceptional. I stayed in one minute walking for three data and ended up having breakfast and dinner every day. Ceviche was out of this world delicious. I really liked how attentive the stuff was, it is incredible like I am in high end Hawaii resort. Great job guys and girls!!! Highly recommend👌🙏"

Sharky's Sports Bar

Every seat in the house is a front row seat! With over 20 TVs and projectors, you’ll be sure to have a great view of your favorite game. We show sports, games, matches and events from all over the world live. Don’t see the game you’re here for? Just ask your bartender and we’ll put it on!

Volcano Brewing Co.

What’s the next best thing after surfing? Great beer, of course! We started brewing beer in 2011 with the goal to locally produce high quality craft beer for our surf camp guests and the rest of the Tamarindo community. You can enjoy quality Volcano Brewing craft beers in our beachfront tasting room and our restaurants Eat at Joe’s and El Vaquero.
